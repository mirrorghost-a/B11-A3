Frequently Asked Questions

What types of pets do you sell?

We offer a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, fish, and small animals like rabbits and hamsters. Availability may vary, so feel free to check with us!

Do you offer pet adoption services?

Yes! We work with local shelters and breeders to help you find a loving pet to adopt.

What pet products do you sell?

We provide a wide range of pet supplies, including food, toys, grooming products, bedding, cages, and health care items.

Can I return or exchange a product if my pet doesn’t like it?

Yes, we accept returns within 14 days for unopened products. Opened products may be eligible for exchange under certain conditions.

Do you have a veterinarian available?

While we don’t have an in-house vet, we partner with local veterinarians and can help you schedule a visit.

Do you offer home delivery for pet supplies?

Yes! We offer home delivery for pet food and supplies. Check our website for delivery areas and charges.
